Diorama is a Collaborative Digital Asset Management for 3D content, that lets you organize, manage, edit and print your 3D assets easily. Its collaborative features enables forks, share print results and social interactions like commenting and sharing. Works with responsive layouts and has a word class ECM, Nuxeo, to back it up. Innovative features like gesture and multitouch modeller, content retrieval and version control takes it to a new level.

Digital Asset Management for 3D

Store all your 3D content in the cloud, organize and manage your assets anytime, anywhere.

Advanced search and powerful 3D retrieval

Find your assets fast and easy, based on fulltext, tags or similarity.

Web based multitouch 3D editor

Intuitive and powerful 3D editor to lead your creativity.

3D printing service integration

Print your 3D creations and materialize your ideas.

Easily print your models locally or online.

Social networks integration

Share your creations with your friends and coworkers.
